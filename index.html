Star Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will ad a specific amout of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the given random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you ntil you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will be randomized.
